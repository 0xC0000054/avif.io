title: Does AVIF support tiling?

---

AVIF supports tiling, which speeds up multicore CPU encoding. After color transforming, the image is divided into tiles, and rectangular areas are separately transformed and encoded. There is no limit to the size of a tile, and the entire image can be considered one tile.
