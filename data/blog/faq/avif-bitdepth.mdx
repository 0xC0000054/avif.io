title: What color bit depth does AVIF support?
description: Read everything you need to know about this question.
category: faq

datePublished: 18.11.21
dateModified: 18.11.21
---

AVIF can support bit depths of 8, 10, and 12 bits per channel. AVIF has a maximum color depth of 12 bits and a maximum number of channels of three.
